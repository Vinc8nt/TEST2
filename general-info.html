📜 History of the MSIT Course

The Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT) program at PUP was established to meet the growing demand for advanced IT professionals in the Philippines and beyond. Since its inception, the program has evolved to include cutting-edge topics such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and cybersecurity.

🎯 What to Expect in the Course

Students enrolled in MSIT can expect a rigorous curriculum that blends theoretical foundations with practical applications. Courses include Software Engineering, Data Analytics, Network Security, and IT Project Management. The program also emphasizes research, innovation, and leadership in the tech industry.
